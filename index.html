Anyone Roleplaying Games

A roleplaying game community in Crown Heights, Brooklyn

AnyoneRPG exists to provide a welcoming, inclusive and supportive environment where longtime players and first-time players can share the joy of roleplaying games together. We work to support an environment where all players can participate, regardless of experience, where players learn through play, where we play roleplaying games in a way that is fun, inventive, collaborative, surprising and, at times, deeply moving.

We host two one-shot games a month, Dungeons & Dragons on the 2nd Sunday of every month, and an independent roleplaying game on last Sunday of every month. We also host a number of short ongoing games on weeknights, that generally last between 6 and 10 sessions. All of our games are open to all; many active members of our community played their first roleplaying game with us. You do not need to own any books, supplies or dice.
