About

Morgan Wajda-levie has worked as an Assistant Film Editor since 2010, and is a member of the Motion Picture Editor's Guild (IATSE Local 700.) Their work spans projects in narrative film, documentary film, scripted television, and commercials.

Morgan has a BA in Russian language and literature from Grinnell College, and has traveled extensively in Russia.
